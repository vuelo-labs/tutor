You've been talking to machines like they're people. Here's what that's costing you — and the only thing you need to do differently.

Step 1 of 3 — See

Two absurdities.
You'll recognise both.

One happens constantly with machines. One happens when the machine shows up in your human conversations.

Human mode — talking to a machine
"Hey! Hope you're having a good day ☺ I was just wondering — and sorry if this is a dumb question — but do you think you could maybe take a look at this data when you get a chance? Absolutely no rush at all, only if you have time! Thank you so much, you're amazing! 🙏"

The greeting. The apology. The hedge. The permission. A complete social performance — for something that was just waiting for the question.

machine mode — talking to a human
Request: lift to airport.
Departure: 02:00.
Origin: home.
Query: available?
Expected output: yes/no.
Deadline: immediate.

// your friend is not a REST API

Both are wrong. Both happen constantly.

The skill is just knowing which one you're in.

Human mode or machine mode?

Read each one. Trust your gut.
